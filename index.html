About Me

Get to know Aditya Shah, WordPress Expert & DevOps Specialist

My name is Aditya Shah, and I absolutely love what I do! I'm passionate about tech, training, and making things run smoothly. With over ten years of experience in WordPress and around 5 years in DevOps and technical support, I love solving complex problems and helping people get the most out of their tools and platforms.
